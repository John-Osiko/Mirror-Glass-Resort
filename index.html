Welcome to Mirror Glass Resort

Explore the natural beauty and simple joys you get when at Lerruat Log resort located at Kumpa in Kajiado County. From all the corners of the lodge, be it at the restaurant, the bar, the cottages or the lodge rooms, be rest assured you will never miss our spectacular and breathtaking views of the rolling hills and valleys.

Whether you are coming over for a business conference, family vacation, group events or a romatic getaway, we have all the amenities to suit your budget and your hearts desires.

We’re open all year round, giving you an opportunity to experience every season in its natural beauty. Welcome!
